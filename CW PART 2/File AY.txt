Angus McKinnon Young is an Australian musician, best known as the co-founder, lead guitarist,songwriter,
and sole constant original member of the Australia hard rock band AC/DC. He is known for his energetic
performances, schoolboy-uinform outfits, an his own version of Chuck Beery's duckwalk.
Young was ranked 24th in Rolling Stone magazine's 100 greatest guitarists of all-time list.
 In 2003, Young and other members of AC/CD were inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame.
